Asked Questions

Can your system predict crop yield on wheat?

Yes! Our crop yield prediction system uses Random Forest Regression model to analyze past yield data, weather conditions, and other factors.

My tomato plants have yellow spots on their leaves. Can your system help me identify the disease and suggest treatments?

Absolutely! Our plant disease prediction system, powered by deep learning (TensorFlow CNN model), can analyze images of affected leaves, Simply upload a clear photo of your tomato plant, and our system will identify possible diseases like early blight or bacterial spots.
